WHO I AM

Farouk Oladega

Software & Cloud Engineer

My mission as a software engineer is to design and build reliable, scalable, and secure applications that solve real-world problems and deliver measurable business value. I specialize in full-stack development with a strong emphasis on cloud engineering, where I enjoy architecting systems that are efficient, resilient, and built to scale.

I am deeply enthusiastic about the cloud and modern infrastructure, particularly AWS, and I continuously explore best practices around cloud-native design, containerization, and deployment automation. I enjoy turning complex technical challenges into clean, maintainable, and user-focused solutions.

Born and raised in Lagos, Nigeria, and based in Canada since 2017, I bring a global perspective that strengthens my adaptability, collaboration, and appreciation for building software that serves diverse users and environments.

I am currently in my final semester of Software Engineering at Centennial College and completed a one-year Software Developer internship with the Ontario Public Service, where I contributed to real-world enterprise systems and workflows. Alongside this, I mentor Software Engineering Technology students while continuously building and refining production-ready personal and freelance projects.
